THE DIVISION

What you rent, what you own.

What you rent

Intelligence

ChatGPT, Claude, and whatever comes next. Rent it monthly and treat it as a replaceable part. Every model upgrade makes your AI smarter — for free.

  • Cloud LLMs (swappable)
  • Reasoning, writing, coding
  • Pay only for what you use

What you own

Memory

Conversation history, knowledge graph, documents, embeddings. Stored on your own Mac — exportable, backed up, and persistent across every model you ever rent.

  • Long-term memory & knowledge graph
  • Documents and RAG indexes
  • Configuration and workflows

Rented intelligence can be replaced.
Your memory compounds.

HOW IT'S BUILT

How it's built

Built on AnythingLLM + Docker, wired to six MCP servers (time, memory, files, search, SQLite, calendar & mail) and local long-term memory. All you need is a Mac and the book's steps. Works on Intel Macs too.
