Author

NOBORu IKUTA

Author of AI of One’s Own and 育てるAI.

NOBORu IKUTA is a Japanese technology builder and author focused on personal AI environments, local knowledge systems, and practical AI workflows for individuals.

His background spans telecommunications infrastructure, network systems, database engineering, and cloud-based data backup services. After years away from the center of the IT industry, he returned to technology by rebuilding a personal AI environment from the ground up.

AI of One’s Own is a record of that reconstruction: not only using AI, but shaping, backing up, extending, and growing an AI environment of one’s own.
