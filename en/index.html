Build your own AI environment, safely and step by step.

This book explains the structure of a personal AI environment: personality, knowledge, memory, tools, documents, Google integration, SQLite, and backups.

NOBORu IKUTA

Author of AI of One’s Own / 育てるAI.

NOBORu IKUTA is a former network and database engineer who returned to building AI systems after years as a full-time father.

From 2019 to 2026, he lived in Malaysia with his three sons. After returning to Japan in 2026, he rebuilt his own personal AI environment with AI as a working partner.

This project documents that process as a book, template pack, official website, and support environment.
