English Kindle Japanese Kindle

About the Kindle editions

AI of One’s Own is the English edition of the project. 育てるAI is the Japanese edition.

Both books explain how to build and grow a personal AI environment: files, memory, tools, Docker, MCP, RAG, SQLite, Google integration, and backup practices.

This is not only a book about using AI. It is a book about building an AI environment of your own.
