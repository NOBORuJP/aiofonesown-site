AI of One’s Own — Early Access Edition is now available on Gumroad

AI of One’s Own — Early Access Edition has been officially published on Gumroad.

View on Gumroad Support site

This English Early Access Edition includes the eBook PDF Ver0.81 and a practical template pack for building a personal AI environment with Docker, MCP, RAG, memory, workspace design, and backup workflows.

The project is based on a simple idea: AI should not only be used as a temporary chat tool. It should be grown, maintained, backed up, and shaped around your own life and work.
